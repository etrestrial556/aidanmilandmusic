About

Aidan Miland is a Minnesota-based saxophonist and composer currently pursuing a Bachelor of Music in Saxophone Performance at Concordia College. With over 11 years of experience, Aidan performs regularly across the state, composing for both jazz and wind ensembles. A multi-instrumentalist, he also plays bass, guitar, flute, clarinet, and drums. Outside of music, Aidan enjoys cooking and biking.
