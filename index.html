About

Aidan Miland is a saxophonist and composer based in Moorhead, Minnesota. He is currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Music Performance at Concordia College, where he continues to develop his musicianship under the guidance of Professor Russell Peterson. With over 11 years of experience on the saxophone, Aidan actively gigs across the Minnesota music scene and composes works for jazz, orchestra, and wind ensembles. He has performed with notable artists including Shaun Johnson and the Fargo Monday Night Big Band.
