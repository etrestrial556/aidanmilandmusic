About

Aidan Miland is a saxophonist and composer based in Minnesota. He is currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Music Performance at Concordia College, where he continues to develop his artistry and musicianship. With over 11 years of experience on the saxophone, Aidan actively gigs across the Minnesota music scene and composes music for both jazz and wind ensembles. Aidan is also a multi-instrumentalist, playing bass, guitar, flute, clarinet, and drums. Outside of music, he enjoys cooking and going on bike rides.
