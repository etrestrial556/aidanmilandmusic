Aidan Miland

Saxophonist • Composer • Multi-Instrumentalist

About

Aidan Miland is a saxophonist and composer based in Moorhead, Minnesota. He is currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Music Performance at Concordia College, where he continues to develop his artistry under the mentorship of Professor Russell Peterson. With over eleven years of experience on the saxophone, Aidan actively performs across the Minnesota music scene and composes works for jazz ensembles, wind bands, and orchestra. He has performed with notable artists and groups, including Shaun Johnson and the Fargo Monday Night Big Band.
